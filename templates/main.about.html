Who I Am

I'm not one to monologue, especially about myself. If you want the short version, I'll describe myself in three words. Artistic. Punny. And I forgot the last one.

However if you must know more about me, I love pasta, think HK-47 is the best companion, have a box full of bubble wrap for rainy days, and enjoy playing Dungeons & Dragons. I think I'm weird, but who isn't? I think what sets me apart from many others are my bad puns. A good joke can make anyone laugh once, but a really bad pun can make everyone cringe forever.

Coding is amazing, but I grew up with drawing. I wanted to study Digital Illustration at the Savannah College of Art and Design. However, since it had no HOPE (the scholarship, ba-dum-tsh), Georgia Tech became my future and broadened my horizon with programming, 3D modeling, animation, and more than I could ever imagine. Whenever videos are rendering or I'm not dying from assignments/lack of sleep, I dabble in creating random stuff.
