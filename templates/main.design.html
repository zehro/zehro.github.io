Design is My Art

Design is not just about making things look nice. It's a way of thinking, a set of principles that can guide people. It's a skill that takes time to master, and many argue about what's best. For me, it's an art, a craft where I can show others how to use things and understand anything. It's one thing to express what I envision, but it's an entirely different challenge to communicate what I have in my head to everyone else. I don't think I'm an expert, but I'd like to think that I'm good at it.

Coding is amazing, but I grew up with drawing. I wanted to study Digital Illustration at the Savannah College of Art and Design. However, since it had no HOPE (the scholarship, ba-dum-tsh), Georgia Tech became my future and broadened my horizon with programming, 3D modeling, animation, and more than I ever thought of. Whenever videos are rendering or I'm not dying from assignments/lack of sleep, I dabble in creating random stuff.
