Design is My Art

Design is not just about making things look nice. It's a way of thinking, a set of principles that can guide people. For me, it's an art, a craft where I can show everyone how to use things and understand anything. It's one thing to express what I envision, but it's an entirely different challenge to communicate with others. Regarding UX, I believe the key is to not think, to promote seamless interaction and grasp people's intuition. I'm no expert, but I'd like to think that I'm good at design.

Beyond graphic design and front-end development, I also draw and try to be creative or something.
