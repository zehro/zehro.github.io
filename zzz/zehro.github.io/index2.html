MY VISION

Coding is amazing. It's like LEGOs, but with computers. I see a whole new world that is limited to my own imagination (and computation). It has its own languages, full of rules and possibilities to create programs and designs that make everyday life more simple with increasing complexity.

On the flip side, I grew up loving art and visual design. I wanted to study fields of Digital Illustration and Graphic Design during high school and planned to go to the Savannah College of Art and Design. However since it has no HOPE (the scholarship, ba-dum-tsh), I broadened my horizon to the more technical, STEM domain.

So with my design background and technical vision, I hope to understand back-end processes to build even better front-end applications with great visual layout. One can program anything in the world, but if it can't be used by anyone, it fails. An application can look great but if it doesn't do anything, it's just an awesome picture. With the two together, it brings success.
